# Test 3.9 — Stage Position Accuracy Results

**Date:** 2026-04-26
**Stage:** THK KR3306A-0500 (6 mm lead, 500 mm stroke)
**Motor:** Kollmorgen AKM21G-ENMN2-00 (2048 PPR encoder, 8192 cnts/rev)
**Test position:** 150 mm from home
**Theoretical resolution:** 0.7324 μm/count (1365.33 cnts/mm)
**Servo gains:** KP=20, KD=100, KI=0.05
**Test speeds:** SPC=5000, ACC=DCC=35000

## Method

Run `demo_accuracy_C.dmc`:
1. Home against negative limit, back off 200 cnts, define `DPC=0`
2. Move to 150 mm test position
3. Mount DTI (±200 μm range) on + side of carriage, preload mid-range
4. Robust backlash takeup: 3× ±1000 cnt cycles
5. Redefine `DPC=0`, zero DTI mid-range
6. 2 warm-up cycles (no recording — settles screw nut)
7. 5 measurement cycles, each visiting:
   - A1: 50 μm target (68 cnts)
   - A2: 100 μm target (137 cnts)
   - A3: 150 μm target (205 cnts)

Each target is approached with a final +100 count move (loads screw nut against + side of backlash zone consistently). 2 s servo settling time before reading position.

## Analysis

### Servo Positioning (encoder vs commanded)

`_TPC` reaches the commanded count exactly on every cycle for every target. Servo following error is **0 counts**.

### Mechanical Accuracy (DTI vs encoder-predicted position)

| Target | `_TPC` (cnts) | Predicted DTI (μm) = `_TPC` × 0.7324 | Measured DTI (μm) | DTI − Predicted (μm) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | 68 | 49.8 | 49 | −0.8 |
| A2 | 137 | 100.4 | 99 | −1.4 |
| A3 | 205 | 150.2 | 149 | −1.2 |

Carriage motion matches encoder motion to within ~1 μm (one DTI minor division). The small consistent ~1 μm shortfall is attributable to either DTI zero offset or sub-micron probe cosine error.

### Repeatability

DTI readings are **identical to ±0 μm across all 5 measurement cycles** for every target. Repeatability is below the DTI's resolution.

### Incremental Accuracy

| Increment | Commanded Δ (μm) | Measured Δ DTI (μm) | Error (μm) |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 → A2 | 50 | 50 | 0 |
| A2 → A3 | 50 | 50 | 0 |

Point-to-point moves are exact within DTI resolution.

## Summary

| Metric | Result | Manufacturer Spec | Pass? |

The stage and servo are performing approximately an order of magnitude better than the manufacturer's repeatability specification. Increasing the integral gain from `KIC=0.001` to `KIC=0.05` eliminated steady-state following error caused by the DTI's spring force loading.
